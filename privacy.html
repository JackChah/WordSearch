WordSearch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What this extension does

WordSearch is a Chrome extension that scans the publicly accessible pages of a website you specify and searches for a word or phrase you provide. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data we collect

WordSearch collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to any external server controlled by us.
  • The URLs and search terms you enter are stored only in your browser's session storage and are cleared when you close the browser.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.

Network access

The extension makes direct HTTP requests from your browser to the website you choose to scan. This is equivalent to visiting those pages yourself. No data from those pages is transmitted anywhere other than your local browser session.

Permissions

WordSearch requests access to all URLs (<all_urls>) solely to fetch the pages of whatever website you choose to scan. This permission is not used to monitor your browsing activity.
